## How to Import a Twilio Phone Number

1. **Signup to Blurr**: If you haven't already, sign up for a Blurr account [here](https://www.blurr.app/auth/signup). Once registered, you'll gain access to the full suite of calling automation features.

2. **Import Twilio Phone Number**: Connect your Twilio phone number to Blurr to start making and receiving calls seamlessly. Follow the instructions below to import your Twilio number.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="circle-info" title="How To Import Twilio Phone Numbers">
  To import your Twilio phone numbers into Blurr, follow these steps:
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion icon="1" title="Buy a Phone Number via Twilio">
        If you don’t already have a number in Twilio, you can purchase one by
        going to your Twilio console’s “Buy a number” section:
        <img src="/images/buy-phone-number-twilio.png" />
        Once you’ve purchased a number, it will immediately be ready for import
        into Blurr.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion icon="2" title="Locate Twilio Account SID & Auth Token">
      To complete the import on Blurr’s side, we will need to grab our Twilio “Account SID” & “Auth Token”.

        You should see a section for “API keys & tokens”, the credentials we will need for the import will live here.

      <img src="/images/twilio-api-key-nav.png" />
      Once we are in our “API keys & tokens” section, we will grab the Account SID & Auth
      Token:

      <img src="/images/twilio-credentials.png" />
      We will use both of these credentials in the next step of importing via the Dashboard.

</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="3" title="Import via Dashboard">
    Navigate to the “Phone Numbers” section & click the “Import” button.
    
    There you will input your phone number, as well as the credentials you retrieved
    in the previous step:

    Your number is now ready to be attached to an assistant for inbound or outbound phone calling.

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

That's it! You've successfully imported a Twilio phone number into Blurr. If you encounter any issues or have any questions, feel free to reach out to our support team for assistance.
