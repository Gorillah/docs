## Eligibility for Refunds

You are eligible for a **full refund** under the following conditions:

- **Technical Issues**: If you experience technical issues that we are unable to resolve within a reasonable time frame.
- **Dissatisfaction with the Service**: If you are not satisfied with our service and provide feedback for your dissatisfaction.
- **Accidental Charges**: If you have been charged accidentally or due to billing errors.

## Refund Request Period

- Refund requests must be made within **30 days** of the payment date.

## How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team by emailing [contact@blurr.app](mailto:contact@blurr.app). Include your account details and the reason for your refund request. For more details on how to reach us, visit our [Contact Us](https://blurr.app/contact) page.

## Refund Processing Time

- Once we receive your refund request, it will be processed within **3 business days**. Please note that it may take additional time for your bank to process the refund and reflect it in your account. Typically, bank processing times may vary from 5 to 10 business days.

## Non-Refundable Charges

- **There are no non-refundable charges**. All eligible refunds are issued as full refunds of the transaction amount.

## Prorated Refunds

- We do not offer prorated refunds for cancellations made in the middle of a billing cycle. Refunds are only issued for the full transaction amount when eligible.

## Documentation

For comprehensive information on our services and detailed documentation on our policies, please refer to [docs.blurr.app](https://docs.blurr.app).

## Exceptions

- **There are no exceptions** to this refund policy. All eligible refund requests that meet the criteria outlined above will be honored.

## Contact Support

For any questions or further assistance, feel free to reach out to our support team at [contact@blurr.app](mailto:contact@blurr.app). We are here to help you with any concerns or issues you may have.

## Additional Information

- **Subscription Management**: You can manage your subscription and billing information through your account dashboard. This includes updating payment methods, viewing transaction history, and canceling subscriptions.
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Your feedback is invaluable to us. If you are requesting a refund due to dissatisfaction, please provide specific details to help us improve our services.
- **Security and Compliance**: We ensure that all transactions are secure and comply with relevant data protection regulations. For more details, refer to our [Privacy Policy](https://blurr.app/privacy) and [Terms of Service](https://blurr.app/).

Thank you for choosing **Blurr App** for your AI calling solutions. We are dedicated to providing you with the best service and support.
